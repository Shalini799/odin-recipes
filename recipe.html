Description

French toast is a dish made by dipping slices of bread in a mixture of beaten eggs and milk (or other liquids), and then frying them until golden brown. It's often served with toppings like syrup, powdered sugar, or fruit. The dish is also known by various other names, including "eggy bread", "poor knights", and "pain perdu".

Description

A sandwich is a food typically consisting of meat, cheese, vegetables, or other fillings placed between two slices of bread. It's a versatile and convenient food, often eaten for lunch or as a snack, and can be prepared at home or purchased from various establishments.

Ingrediants

  • bread
  • meat
  • vegetables

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients you need for your sandwich
  2. Take two slices of bread
  3. On one or both slices of bread, spread your desired condiments
  4. Add vegetables of your choice on one of the bread slices
  5. Add your chosen meat if you want to have a non-vegetarian bite.
  6. To add creaminess and extra flavour, you can add your preferred type of cheese.
  7. If you like, you can cut your sandwich in half diagonally for a classic presentation.
